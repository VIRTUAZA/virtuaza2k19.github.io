ABOUT INSTITUTE 

  

	
					Hindusthan College Of Engineering and Technology was started in the year 2000 by
			the great industralist and philanthropist Thiru.T.S.R.Khannaiyan.The primary objective of
			Hindusthan college of Engineering is to educate and prepare men and women for leadership 
			industry,government,and educational institutions, to advance the knowldge base of engineering
			professions and to influence the future directions of engineering education and practice.

ABOUT DEPARTMENT 

  






					The department of Electronics and Communication Engineering  has its origin 
			in the year 2001.The department offers BE programme and 2 ME programmes(Communication 
			system and ECE) with a vision to produce globally competent professionals.The department
			has highly qualified and rich experienced faculty members coupled with younger talents and 
			supporting staff.Within this 15 years of experiance it has achieved several milestones
			in its widespread academic activities which include securing appreciabe count of university
			ranks consistently every academic year.

ABOUT FEST 

  
 
 















						VIRTUAZA'19 is A National Level Technical Symposium organized by our
